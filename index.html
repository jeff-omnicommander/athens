Athens Area Credit Union is a not-for-profit, member owned cooperative.

Based in Athens, Wisconsin, the Athens Area Credit Union is a not-for-profit, member owned cooperative. Our goal is to provide the best possible financial services at competitive rates to meet the needs of our members.

Get everything you find at a bank – savings, checking, CDs, IRAs, HSAs to personal, automobile and real estate loans – without all the fees. Athens residents and those in the surrounding communities have relied on us to provide “The Credit Union Difference” for more than 57 years. The familiar faces of our friendly staff always provide our members with exceptional customer service.
